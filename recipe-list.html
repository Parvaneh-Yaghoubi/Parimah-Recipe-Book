  • Desserts

    • Sheer Pira

      Sheer Pira, a delicious milk fudge that melts in your mouth, has been a favorite in Afghanistan for centuries. This sweet and creamy treat is made by slowly cooking down condensed milk, sugar, and ghee until it reaches the perfect texture. The result is a rich caramel-colored fudge that is topped with chopped nuts and dried fruits which add a bit of texture and crunch. With each bite, the flavors explode in your mouth, leaving you feeling satisfied yet craving more.

    • Sheer Yakh

      Are you a fan of ice cream? Then allow me to introduce you to a flavor that is sure to transport your taste buds to a whole new level "Afghani Sheer Yakh". You've never tasted ice cream like this! This exotic treat has a unique flavor profile thanks to saffron, rose water, and pistachios. But what really sets it apart is the addition of creamy evaporated milk that gives this frozen dessert a rich and luxurious texture.

    • Gosh-e Fil

      If you're in the market for something sweet, delicious, and a bit exotic, I've got just the thing: "Afghani Gosh-e Fil"! These quirky treats are also known as elephant ears and you just might see why in the photo! This traditional dessert from Afghanistan consists of light, flaky pastry shells filled with a sweet and creamy mixture of milk, sugar, and cardamom. The result is a mouth-watering treat that is both decadent and delicate.
