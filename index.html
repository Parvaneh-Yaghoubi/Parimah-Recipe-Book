 Bolani

 

Bolani is a traditional Afghan stuffed flatbread, pan-fried to golden perfection. Filled with savory ingredients like potatoes, leeks, or lentils, it's crispy on the outside and soft, flavorful on the inside. perfect as a snack, appetizer, or light meal.
